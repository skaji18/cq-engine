# Service Agreement

## Section 1: Definitions

"Service" means the cloud hosting platform provided by Provider.
"Customer Data" means all data uploaded by Customer to the Service.

## Section 2: Service Levels

Provider shall use reasonable efforts to maintain 99.9% uptime for all production services.
Provider shall respond to critical incidents in a timely manner.
The SLA target is 30 days response time for non-critical issues.

## Section 3: Security

Provider shall implement appropriate security measures to protect Customer Data.
All data must be encrypted. Provider certifies compliance with industry standard practices.

## Section 4: Payment

Customer shall pay invoices within 30 days of receipt.
Late payments shall incur a penalty of 1.5 percent per month.

## Section 5: Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement by providing 30 days written notice.
This agreement shall remain in effect for 12 months from the effective date.

## Section 6: Breach

In the event of material breach, the non-breaching party may terminate immediately.
Provider shall not be liable for indirect damages.
Provider shall be liable for all direct damages caused by breach.

## Section 7: Miscellaneous

This section contains general boilerplate language about the agreement.
The parties agree to act in good faith.
